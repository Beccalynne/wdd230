Design Principles Document

Becca Norman

Contrast

Crumbl Website

In this website, Crumbl use contrast with the background being black and the text white for a lot of the links. The background is primarily white making the black background stand out more so.

Repetition

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints Website

Within the LDS.org website the links are placed in a way that is very repetitive.

Alignment

MixHers Website

For the mobile view of this website all the words and sections are all center aligned. This wasn't something that we are taught to do often unless it is intentional. The text is aligned center but so are the sections and pictures.
